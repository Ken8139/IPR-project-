Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is VitalTrack Pro's heart rate monitoring?
VitalTrack Pro uses advanced PPG sensors and AI algorithms to achieve 99.9% accuracy in heart rate monitoring. Our technology is clinically validated and compared against medical-grade ECG devices.
How long does the battery last?
The VitalTrack Pro device typically lasts up to 7 days on a single charge with normal usage. Battery life may vary based on feature usage and settings.
Is my health data secure?
Yes, we take data security seriously. All health data is encrypted using military-grade encryption and stored in HIPAA-compliant servers. You have full control over your data sharing preferences.
Can I share my health data with my doctor?
Yes, VitalTrack Pro allows you to generate detailed health reports that you can share with your healthcare providers. You can also grant direct access to your data through our healthcare provider portal.
