Initial Pairing Process

  1. 1 Prepare Your Device:
    • Ensure your VitalTrack Pro is fully charged
    • Power on the device
    • Place it within 3 feet of your smartphone
    • Open the VitalTrack Pro app
  2. 2 Enable Bluetooth:
    • Open your smartphone's Settings
    • Navigate to Bluetooth settings
    • Turn on Bluetooth
    • Make your device discoverable
  3. 3 Start Pairing:
    • In the app, tap "Add New Device"
    • Select "VitalTrack Pro" from the list
    • Enter the pairing code if prompted
    • Wait for the connection confirmation
Pro Tip: Keep your device and smartphone close together during the pairing process to ensure a strong connection.

Supported Devices

  • iOS Devices

    iPhone 8 and newer, running iOS 13 or later

  • Android Devices

    Android 8.0 or later with Bluetooth 4.0+

  • Tablets

    iPad and Android tablets with Bluetooth support

Reconnection Process

  1. 1 Automatic Reconnection:
    • Device will automatically reconnect when in range
    • Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone
    • Keep the app running in the background
    • Check device battery level
  2. 2 Manual Reconnection:
    • Open the VitalTrack Pro app
    • Go to Settings > Device Connection
    • Select your device from the list
    • Tap "Connect"

Troubleshooting Bluetooth Issues

  1. 1 Connection Problems:
    • Restart both devices
    • Check Bluetooth is enabled
    • Verify device compatibility
    • Update device firmware
  2. 2 Signal Issues:
    • Reduce distance between devices
    • Remove potential interference
    • Check for other Bluetooth devices
    • Reset Bluetooth settings
  3. 3 App Issues:
    • Update the app to latest version
    • Clear app cache and data
    • Reinstall the app if needed
    • Check app permissions
