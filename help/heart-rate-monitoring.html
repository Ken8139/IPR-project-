Understanding Heart Rate

Resting Heart Rate

Your heart rate when at complete rest

Normal Range: 60-100 bpm

Maximum Heart Rate

Highest heart rate during intense exercise

Estimated: 220 - your age

Heart Rate Variability

Variation in time between heartbeats

Higher values indicate better recovery

Pro Tip: For accurate readings, ensure the device is properly positioned and snug on your wrist.

Heart Rate Zones

Zone Intensity Percentage of Max HR Benefits
Zone 1 Very Light 50-60% Recovery, warm-up
