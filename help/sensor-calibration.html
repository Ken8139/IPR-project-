Heart Rate Calibration

Initial Setup

Calibrate your heart rate sensor:

  1. Wear the device properly
  2. Ensure clean sensor surface
  3. Stay still for 30 seconds
  4. Follow on-screen instructions
  5. Verify calibration results

Calibration Tips

For best results:

  • Wear device snugly but comfortably
  • Avoid movement during calibration
  • Ensure proper skin contact
  • Calibrate in a stable environment
Pro Tip: For optimal heart rate accuracy, calibrate your device after any significant changes in your activity level or fitness routine.

Activity Tracking Calibration

Step Calibration

Calibrate your step counter:

  1. Walk 100 steps at normal pace
  2. Enter actual step count
  3. Repeat for different speeds
  4. Save calibration settings
  5. Verify accuracy

Activity Recognition

Improve activity detection:

  • Perform each activity type
  • Set activity intensity levels
  • Configure auto-detection
  • Update personal metrics

Sleep Tracking Calibration

Sleep Detection

Calibrate sleep tracking:

  1. Set your sleep schedule
  2. Wear device during sleep
  3. Record sleep patterns
  4. Adjust sensitivity settings
  5. Verify sleep stage detection

Sleep Quality

Optimize sleep tracking:

  • Set sleep goals
  • Configure sleep reminders
  • Adjust motion sensitivity
  • Enable smart alarm

Temperature Calibration

Body Temperature

Calibrate temperature sensor:

  1. Ensure clean sensor surface
  2. Wear device for 15 minutes
  3. Compare with reference
  4. Adjust calibration offset
  5. Save temperature settings

Environmental Factors

Consider these factors:

  • Room temperature
  • Device placement
  • Activity level
  • Time of day
