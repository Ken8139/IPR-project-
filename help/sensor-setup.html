Initial Sensor Setup

  1. 1 Prepare Your Device:
    • Ensure your VitalTrack Pro is fully charged
    • Clean the sensor area with a soft cloth
    • Remove any protective film from sensors
    • Open the VitalTrack Pro app
  2. 2 Sensor Calibration:
    • Go to Settings > Sensors
    • Select "Calibrate Sensors"
    • Follow on-screen instructions
    • Wait for calibration to complete
  3. 3 Verify Setup:
    • Check sensor readings
    • Verify accuracy with test measurements
    • Adjust sensitivity if needed
    • Save calibration settings
Pro Tip: For best results, calibrate your sensors in a stable environment with moderate temperature and humidity.

Maintenance and Care

  1. 1 Regular Maintenance:
    • Clean sensors daily
    • Check for physical damage
    • Update firmware regularly
    • Perform periodic calibration
  2. 2 Storage and Handling:
    • Store in protective case
    • Avoid extreme temperatures
    • Keep away from moisture
    • Handle with clean hands

Troubleshooting Sensor Issues

  1. 1 Common Problems:
    • Inaccurate readings
    • Sensor not responding
    • Connection issues
    • Battery drain
  2. 2 Solutions:
    • Re-calibrate sensors
    • Clean sensor area
    • Restart device
    • Update firmware
  3. 3 Advanced Troubleshooting:
    • Check sensor alignment
    • Verify software settings
    • Test in different conditions
    • Contact support if needed
