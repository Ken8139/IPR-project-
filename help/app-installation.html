System Requirements

iOS Requirements:

  • iOS 14.0 or later
  • iPhone 8 or newer
  • iPad 5th generation or newer
  • 2GB of available storage

Android Requirements:

  • Android 10.0 or later
  • 2GB RAM minimum
  • 2GB of available storage
  • Bluetooth 4.0 or later

Step 1: Download the App

  1. 1 For iOS devices:
    • Open the App Store
    • Search for "VitalTrack Pro"
    • Tap "Get" to download
    • Authenticate with Face ID/Touch ID if prompted
  2. 2 For Android devices:
    • Open the Google Play Store
    • Search for "VitalTrack Pro"
    • Tap "Install"
    • Accept the required permissions
Pro Tip: Ensure you have a stable internet connection during the download process. The app size is approximately 150MB.

Step 2: Initial Setup

  1. 1 Launch the app:
    • Open the VitalTrack Pro app
    • Accept the Terms of Service
    • Grant necessary permissions
    • Create or sign in to your account
  2. 2 Configure basic settings:
    • Set your preferred language
    • Choose measurement units
    • Configure notification preferences
    • Set up privacy settings

Step 3: App Permissions

  1. 1 Required permissions:
    • Bluetooth access for device connection
    • Location services for accurate tracking
    • Notifications for alerts and updates
    • Camera access for QR code scanning
  2. 2 Optional permissions:
    • Health app integration
    • Calendar access for scheduling
    • Photo library for profile pictures
    • Microphone for voice commands

Troubleshooting Common Issues

  1. 1 Installation fails:
    • Check internet connection
    • Verify device storage space
    • Update device operating system
    • Restart your device
  2. 2 App crashes:
    • Clear app cache and data
    • Reinstall the app
    • Check for app updates
    • Update device software
  3. 3 Permission issues:
    • Check device settings
    • Enable required permissions
    • Restart the app
    • Reinstall if necessary
