Wearing Positions

  • Wrist Position

    Wear on the non-dominant wrist, one finger width above the wrist bone

  • Sensor Contact

    Ensure sensors maintain good skin contact

  • Band Fit

    Adjust band to be snug but comfortable

Wearing Instructions

  1. 1 Initial Setup:
    • Clean your wrist area
    • Adjust band to proper length
    • Position device correctly
    • Secure the band
  2. 2 Daily Wear:
    • Wear continuously for best results
    • Adjust for comfort as needed
    • Keep sensors clean
    • Monitor skin condition
Pro Tip: For best results, wear the device consistently in the same position and ensure the sensors maintain good contact with your skin.

Comfort and Fit

  1. 1 Band Adjustment:
    • Adjust for snug fit
    • Allow for slight movement
    • Check for skin irritation
    • Ensure proper circulation
  2. 2 Comfort Tips:
    • Take breaks if needed
    • Clean band regularly
    • Alternate wrists if desired
    • Monitor skin health

Activity-Specific Wearing

  1. 1 Exercise:
    • Secure band tighter during activity
    • Check sensor contact
    • Clean after sweating
    • Monitor for discomfort
  2. 2 Sleep:
    • Ensure comfortable fit
    • Check for proper contact
    • Clean before bed
    • Monitor sleep position

Troubleshooting Wearing Issues

  1. 1 Common Problems:
    • Skin irritation
    • Poor sensor contact
    • Inaccurate readings
    • Discomfort
  2. 2 Solutions:
    • Adjust band tightness
    • Clean device regularly
    • Try different positions
    • Take regular breaks
  3. 3 Care Instructions:
    • Clean device daily
    • Inspect for damage
    • Check band condition
    • Monitor skin health
