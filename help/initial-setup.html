Unboxing and Initial Setup

  1. 1 Package Contents:
    • VitalTrack Pro device
    • Charging cable
    • User manual
    • Warranty card
    • Quick start guide
  2. 2 Initial Charging:
    • Connect the charging cable to the device
    • Plug into a power source
    • Wait for the battery indicator to show full charge
    • This may take up to 2 hours
  3. 3 Device Preparation:
    • Power on the device
    • Check for any visible damage
    • Ensure all components are working
    • Prepare for app installation
Pro Tip: Keep the device charging while you set up the app to ensure it's ready for immediate use.

App Installation and Setup

  1. 1 Download the App:
    • Visit the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android)
    • Search for "VitalTrack Pro"
    • Download and install the app
    • Open the app once installation is complete
  2. 2 Account Creation:
    • Enter your email address
    • Create a secure password
    • Verify your email
    • Complete your profile information
  3. 3 Initial Configuration:
    • Set your preferred language
    • Choose your measurement units
    • Configure notification preferences
    • Set up emergency contacts

Device Pairing and Calibration

  1. 1 Bluetooth Connection:
    • Enable Bluetooth on your smartphone
    • Open the VitalTrack Pro app
    • Select "Add New Device"
    • Follow the on-screen instructions
  2. 2 Device Calibration:
    • Wear the device properly
    • Follow the calibration prompts
    • Remain still during calibration
    • Wait for confirmation of successful calibration
  3. 3 Initial Testing:
    • Check heart rate monitoring
    • Verify step counting
    • Test sleep tracking
    • Confirm notification delivery

Troubleshooting Common Issues

  1. 1 Connection Problems:
    • Ensure Bluetooth is enabled
    • Check device proximity
    • Restart both devices
    • Update app and device firmware
  2. 2 Calibration Issues:
    • Ensure proper device placement
    • Check for interference
    • Repeat calibration process
    • Contact support if problems persist
  3. 3 Battery Concerns:
    • Verify charging connection
    • Check power source
    • Monitor battery health
    • Follow charging best practices
