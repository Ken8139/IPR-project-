Battery Life Optimization

Display Settings

Optimize your display settings to save battery:

  • Reduce screen brightness
  • Enable auto-brightness
  • Shorten screen timeout
  • Use dark mode when available

Feature Management

Manage device features to conserve battery:

  • Disable unused sensors
  • Turn off unnecessary notifications
  • Limit background app refresh
  • Use airplane mode when possible
Pro Tip: For optimal battery life, keep your device's battery level between 20% and 80% and avoid exposing it to extreme temperatures.

Charging Best Practices

Optimal Charging

Follow these charging guidelines:

  1. Use the original charger
  2. Charge in a cool environment
  3. Avoid overcharging
  4. Unplug when fully charged
  5. Clean charging contacts regularly

Battery Health

Maintain battery health:

  • Perform regular full discharges
  • Update device firmware
  • Store device with 50% charge
  • Replace battery if needed
