Initial WiFi Setup

  1. 1 Prepare Your Device:
    • Ensure your VitalTrack Pro is powered on
    • Make sure you're in range of your WiFi network
    • Have your WiFi password ready
    • Open the VitalTrack Pro app
  2. 2 Access WiFi Settings:
    • Go to Settings > Network
    • Select "WiFi Setup"
    • Choose your network from the list
    • Enter your WiFi password
  3. 3 Complete Setup:
    • Wait for connection confirmation
    • Verify network status
    • Test connection speed
    • Save network settings
Pro Tip: For best performance, place your device within 30 feet of your WiFi router and avoid physical obstructions.
