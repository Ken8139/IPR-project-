Understanding Sleep Stages

Light Sleep

Transition between wakefulness and sleep

Duration: 10-30 minutes

Deep Sleep

Restorative sleep for physical recovery

Duration: 20-40% of total sleep

REM Sleep

Dream sleep for mental recovery

Duration: 20-25% of total sleep

Pro Tip: For accurate sleep tracking, wear your device consistently and ensure it's properly positioned on your wrist.
