Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

Is there a long-term contract?

No, all our plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment required.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise plans.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for both Basic and Professional plans.

Can I add family members to my plan?

Yes, the Professional plan includes up to 3 family members, and the Enterprise plan includes unlimited family members. You can also add more family members to the Professional plan for an additional fee.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access to your plan until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer discounts for healthcare providers?

Yes, we offer special pricing for healthcare providers and organizations. Please contact our sales team for more information.

Is my health data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your health data. All data is stored in secure, HIPAA-compliant servers.
